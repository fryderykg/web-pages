WORK

When not photographing for work,
I'm still documenting life.

As a photographer there is nothing more important to me than capturing great images that tell a story, trigger a memory and evoke emotion. My goal is to capture YOU guys and capture it in artistic and fun way.

I want to bring you super fun, happy, honest and photojournalistic pictures that you will be proud to share on facebook page with you firends and family.

ABOUT

Whatever Souls Are Made Of

I blink my eyes open each morning, inch out of bed, and pull back the window shades, ensuring the light that leaks into the room avoids my husband's eyes. The early sun smiles and I take a deep breath. In this moment, I realize I have the best job in the world. I document life.

I delight in simple pleasures like take a cup of coffee in the morning, having a meal with friends, walking in the woods, visiting crowded cities, long drives going nowhere, meeting new people.
